Projects

  EventerZgz Winning Android project of the year 2015 hackathon in Zaragoza Application made with MVP architecture.
Through EventerZgz you can customize your searches to easily obtain the events that interest you most.
You will get all the information of the event, being able to share it in the social networks, add it to your calendar, visit the website of the same or call the phone to obtain more information. Also if you do not know how to get to the event, EventerZgz helps you by telling you how you can get to the event.
